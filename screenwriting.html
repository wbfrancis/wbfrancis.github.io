Letters To A Young Poet, hour-long TV drama pilot
  • Co-written with Jackson Prince, this pilot adapts the poet Ranier Maria Rilke's famous correspondence with a young poet into a modern context. The pilot follows Ray Kappus, a young poet and singer-songwriter in New York City as he begins a transatlantic correspondence with the renowned poet Hugo Aguilar, currently living in Basque Spain. Through their relationship, both Ray and Hugo's reality becomes suffused with fantasy and magic, as it seems the logic of their poetry is leaking into the real world.

Culty, hour-long TV comedy pilot
  • An off-beat comedy that follows Tom, an atheist vlogger who moves in with his brother after being kicked out of his mom's basement. Turns out though, that his brother has joined a strange group that frequently meets at the local community center. Tom attends one of their "services", lead by a mysterious figure who calls himself Yeshua. It's all very... well, culty.

Hunt, short film
  • HUNT follows the young Jack, who, for his 13th birthday, gets dragged along on a hunting trip with his Pawpaw, a severe man and seasoned hunter. Jack's never hunted before, but Pawpaw's determined that he get his first kill, as a kind of rite of manhood.
